# Fuel and Cost Calculations

This document describes how fuel usage and operating costs are calculated.

## Fuel Burn Rates
- Boeing 737-600: 850 gal/hr
- Boeing 737-800: 950 gal/hr
- Airbus A220-100: 700 gal/hr
- Airbus A220-300: 800 gal/hr

Fuel burn applies only to airborne time.

## Fuel Cost
### United States
- $6.19 per gallon

### France
- $2.29 per liter
- Conversion: 1 gallon = 3.785 liters

Fuel cost is calculated based on the location where fuel is purchased.

## Airport Fees
### United States
- $2,000 per takeoff
- $2,000 per landing

### France
- $2,450 per takeoff or landing

## Total Operating Cost
total cost =
fuel cost + takeoff fees + landing fees
